Pizza

Delicious Veggie Pizza

A pizza is an Italian dish enjoyed by many around the world. It is one of the most popular dishes in the world with each country adding their own twist to the recipe to match their taste. It is extremely popular in North America and you can easily find a pizza shop where ever you are. It is also relatively inexpensive compared to other dishes and can be quite filling for a large group of people.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of store-bought pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup of chopped tomatoes
  • 1/3 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup of black olives

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 500 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Spread pizza dough evenly on a pizza pan
  3. Spread pizza sauce on the pizza leaving about 1 inch off the edges
  4. Generously cover the pizza with cheese
  5. Spread your toppings on the pizza
  6. Bake pizza until the cheese is bubbling and the crust is golden
  7. Slice into 6-8 pieces and serve
