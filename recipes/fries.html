Fries Recipe

Spicy Fries

Fries are a simple yet delicious dish. It goes well with a lot of other foods hence why we see them in a lot of fast food places. They are easy to make and can be a delicious snack by itself or as a side-dish. This recipe is going to spice things up a bit by adding some spice to our fires

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Oil for Deep frying
  • Salt
  • Chili Powder
  1. Thoroughly wash the potatoes and make sure the dirt is off the skin
  2. Peel the potatoes
  3. Cut the potato in thick slices
  4. Heat the oil in a frying pan
  5. Gently slide the fries in the frying pan until golden brown
  6. Remove from oil and place on kitchen paper to draw out excess oil
  7. top it off with some salt and chili powder
