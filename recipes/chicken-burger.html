Chicken Burger

A chicken burger is one of the most iconic dishes around the world. Different countries have created different interpretations of the burger which all taste delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken breast
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • Lemon juice
  • Onions
  • chives
  • Mayo
  • Salt and pepper
  • Bread crumbs
  • Burger Buns
  • cheese slice

Steps

  1. Make the sauce by mixing mayo, lemon juice, and salt and pepper
  2. Oil and pre-heat the grill at medium heat
  3. chop the chives and onions
  4. In a bowl, add the chicken breast, lemon juice, chives, bread crumbs, mayo, salt & pepper
  5. let the chicken marinate for at least 30 mins
  6. Slice the chicken breast in half if it is too big
  7. Gently place the chicken breast on the grill until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees fahrenheit
  8. Once the Chicken breast is cooked, cook the burger buns for about 30 seconds
  9. Asseble the burger by adding the chicken breast, onions, mayo, cheese slice
